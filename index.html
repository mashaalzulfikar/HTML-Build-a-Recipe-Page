Chocolate Cake Shake Recipe

A chocolate cake shake is a creamy dessert made by blending chocolate cake, chocolate ice cream, and milk together. It's a delicious way to enjoy cake in a drinkable form, often topped with whipped cream and chocolate sprinkles.

Ingredients

  • Chocolate cake
  • Chocolate ice cream
  • Milk
  • Whipped cream

Instructions

  1. Blend together 2 cups of leftover chocolate cake, 2 cups of chilled milk, and 2 scoops of chocolate or vanilla ice cream until smooth.
  2. If desired, adjust the thickness by adding more milk for a thinner shake or less for a thicker consistency.
  3. Pour the mixture into a tall glass and garnish with whipped cream or chocolate sauce if you like.
  4. erve immediately and enjoy your delicious chocolate cake shake!
